Internship Pathway

Gain real cybersecurity experience with hands-on internship support.

OffShield connects learners to practical projects, mentorship, and placement-ready opportunities.

Structured mentorship

Work alongside experienced security professionals while learning how teams solve real incidents.

Practical project work

Complete assessments, reports, and security reviews that mirror the demands of live cybersecurity roles.

Placement support

Get guidance on internships, interview preparation, and professional presentation to help you succeed.

Who should apply?

Students, new graduates, and career switchers who have completed cybersecurity training and want on-the-job experience.

  • Structured mentorship and coaching
  • Practical tasks aligned to real security work
  • Experience with documentation, reports, and assessments
  • Career guidance and placement support

How it works

  1. Enroll in a training program or apply with your existing skills.
  2. Complete guided projects and internship readiness assessments.
  3. Receive placement support with real industry partners.
  4. Secure your first professional cybersecurity role with confidence.
